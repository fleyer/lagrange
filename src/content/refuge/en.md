My heart's desire is to offer you a time of rest, relaxation, and above all, a moment of joy.

Modest and simple, La Grange de Marie France provides everything you need for a pleasant evening and a restorative night on the Way. Here, every pilgrim is welcomed warmly, no matter how far they have walked.
